About Company

15 Years of Experience Now in Bulgaria

Tarlex company started its operations in Ukraine in 2009 and over 15 years has become a recognized leader in water treatment and chemical water purification system modernization.

Today we are opening a new chapter of our history in Bulgaria, bringing accumulated experience, innovative technologies and proven solutions to the Bulgarian market.

Our work principles remain unchanged: maximum reliability, energy efficiency and technology in every project. We guarantee results and take full responsibility for the quality of our solutions.
